Heartfelt

The final project developed on semester on DADIU, with 16 other students. Heartfelt is an isometric puzzle game, where you play as a boy. He fights with his emotions, represented as enemies, which he has to fight with his heart, showing vulnerbility. The core mechanic is that your biggest advantage is also your weakest point.
