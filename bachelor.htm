AI bot for Starcraft: Brood War

Thesis project for my bachelor in software technology from DTU. Developing a bot for the RTS game Starcraft: Brood War to play against other bots.

The bot was made using BWAPI, an open source framework using C++, made for hobbyist, students and researchers to make Articifial Intelliegence agent that can play Starcraft: Brood War.

The goal of the project was to make a dynamic bot, by making it able to adabpt to different strategies of the opponent, in real-time. We used heurestics to calculate and plan a build order to counter the opponent. The micro management of the military units was inspiret by Hierarchical Task Networks. Furthermore the bot was planned to work with all three playable races in the game.

The bot was not as successful as the former project we made for our software technology project. But in comparison the former bot only focussed on a single race, Protoss, which is also the "easiest" race for beginners of the game.
