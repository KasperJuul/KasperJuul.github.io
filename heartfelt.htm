Heartfelt

Isometric puzzle game, where your advantage is also your weakest point. A game about a young man going through the emotional process of being in a relationship.

The final project made during my participation in the DADIU programme. Students gets matched in teams having all the competences needed for developing a game such as game director, game designer, leveldesigner, CG artists etc.

The game uses only a couple of simple puzzle mechanics. But these are reused in all the levels in different ways. Enemies are monsters representing different emotions the main character is going through, and has their own behavior. The player must learn this behavior and use it to his own advantage.

The core mechanic in the game is shooting the players heart to trigger buttons or lure enemies into traps. Enemies are drawn to the heart and not the main character himself. The heart is also the players weakpoint, from where damage will be taken. So the player must act carefully, to not just feed the heart to the bad emotions.

This tought me to work in a team spanding over a lot of different competences, i learned how to communicate across these.
