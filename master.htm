Deep Dungeon Adventure

A top-down 2D dungeon crawler, made as part of my master thesis on dynamic difficulty adjustment in games.

The game is pretty simple featurewise as it melee combat an a single pickup (health). The main focus on the game was DDA.

The system for DDA is heavily inspiret by the studies of Robin Hunicke, who made a DDA system called HAMLET for a game made with the GoldSrc engine. The system uses probability theory, to calculate the amount of challenge the player is experiencing. This is measured by the probability of the player being hit by having a normal distribution on the damage taken.
